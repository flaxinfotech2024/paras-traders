Enterprise Solutions

We provide a wide range of diamond powders, including RVD Green, Yellow Synthetic, Micron, Resin Bond, and Synthetic Industrial Diamond Powder, all crafted to meet the highest standards of quality and precision for various industrial needs.

RVD Green
Diamond Powder

Diamond powder is made of diamond crystal, it is with straight crystal edge, bright crystal surface and transparent yellow colour,also has high strength and thermal stability. Raw material is adopted from high technology RVD series of high quality diamonds, processed by special crushing technology, it has irregular edge shaped particles.

Micron
Diamond Powder

Diamond micron powder finds extensive use in industries such as electronics, optics, telecommunications, and precision machining. It is commonly employed in processes like lapping, polishing, and superfinishing of various materials, including metals, ceramics, glass, and semiconductors.

Resin Bond
Diamond Powder

Resin bond diamond powder is a versatile and high-performance material used in various industries for cutting, grinding, and polishing applications. It is composed of high-quality synthetic diamonds that are carefully bonded together with a resin matrix, creating a powerful abrasive tool that offers unparalleled precision and efficiency.

Synthetic Industrial
Diamond Powder

Diamond abrasives are commonly classed as superabrasives due to their extreme toughness when compared with other abrasive materials. It is these unique properties that set diamond apart and make it such an important ingredient in advanced grinding operations.

Yellow Synthetic
Diamond Powder

Yellow Synthetic Diamond Powder is a processed synthetic diamond abrasive with a consistent regular shape and comparatively complete crystal shape. It's Raw material is adopted from high technology RVD series of high quality diamonds.
