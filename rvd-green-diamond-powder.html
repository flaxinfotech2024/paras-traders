Description

RVD green diamond powder is a synthetic diamond powder that is used in a variety of industrial applications.

RVD stands for Resin Bond Diamond, and the powder is designed for use in resin bond applications such as:


Diamond tools like saw blades, grinding wheels, and drill bits.

Diamond-coated abrasives like sandpaper and sanding belts.

Diamond-filled polymers like adhesives and composites .
